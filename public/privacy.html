Virtual Stylist AI — Privacy & Cookies Notice

Effective date: 28 January 2026

1) Who we are

Virtual Stylist AI (“we”, “us”) provides AI-powered outfit recommendations. Contact: privacy@virtual-stylist.ai.

2) Data we collect

  • Account basics: name, email, referral code.
  • Usage: actions in the app, language and theme preferences.
  • Content you provide: uploaded outfit photos, prompts, messages.
  • Device & diagnostics: browser/OS, app version, coarse location if you grant permission for nearby-store search.

3) Why we collect it

Service delivery

Generate outfits, manage loyalty points, run referrals, show nearby stores.

Security

Detect abuse and keep the service reliable.

Improvement

Diagnostics and aggregated analytics to improve quality; no ads profiling.

4) Legal bases (GDPR)

  • Contract: to provide styling features you request.
  • Consent: location access, marketing emails, optional cookies.
  • Legitimate interest: security and fraud prevention.

5) Sharing

  • Infrastructure and analytics providers (e.g., hosting, error logging) bound by DPAs.
  • No sale of personal data. No third-party ads networks.
  • Compliance: if required by law or to protect rights.

6) Cookies & storage

  • Essential: session state, language, theme, loyalty cache (first-party, strictly necessary).
  • Analytics (optional where enabled): performance metrics; disabled until consent where required.
  • You can clear cookies/localStorage via browser settings; essential cookies are needed for core features.

7) Data retention

Account and loyalty records are kept while your account is active. Uploads and chat history are retained until you delete them or clear your profile. Backups follow rolling retention for disaster recovery.

8) Your rights

Depending on your region: access, rectification, deletion, restriction, objection, portability, and withdrawal of consent. To exercise, email privacy@virtual-stylist.ai.

9) International transfers

Data may be processed in the US/EU. We use standard contractual clauses or equivalent safeguards where applicable.

10) Children

The service is not intended for users under 16. If you believe a minor used the service, contact us to remove their data.

11) Updates

We will post updates here and change the “Effective date”. Continued use means acceptance of the updated notice.
